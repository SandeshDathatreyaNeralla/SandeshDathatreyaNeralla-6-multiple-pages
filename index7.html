Our Tiffins

Dosa

A dosa, also called dosai and dosaka, is a thin pancake in South Indian cuisine made from a fermented batter of ground black lentils .

Parotta

Parotta or Porotta is a Subcontinental layered Indian flatbread made from Maida or Atta, alternatively known as flaky ribbon pancake.

Vada

Vada is a category of savoury fried snacks native to South India. Vadas can be described variously as fritters, cutlets, doughnuts .

Idli

Idli or idly is a type of savoury rice cake, originating from the Indian subcontinent, popular as breakfast foods in Southern India.
